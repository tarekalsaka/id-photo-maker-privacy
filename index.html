Privacy Policy

Official ID Photo Maker

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the ID Photo Maker mobile application developed by Tarek Alsaka. We are fundamentally committed to your privacy. This application was engineered from the ground up using a "Zero-Retention Architecture," meaning your biometric data remains yours alone.

2. Camera & Photo Access

Our application requires access to your device's camera and photo gallery strictly to capture or select a portrait for ID photo processing.

  • Real-time Processing: Images are captured and temporarily held in volatile memory (RAM) solely for the duration of the AI background removal and biometric cropping process.
  • No Background Access: We only access your camera when you explicitly press the capture button.

3. Zero-Retention Data Processing

We do not store, save, or harvest your photographs.

  • Photos transmitted to our processing servers are analyzed dynamically and converted immediately into an encrypted Base64 image stream.
  • Once the processed image is returned to your device, the original file and all associated data are instantly permanently wiped from our server memory.
  • No images are ever written to a database or hard drive.

4. GDPR & EU Data Sovereignty

Designed and deployed in accordance with strict European Union data protection regulations (GDPR), ID Photo Maker does not engage in user profiling, tracking, or the sale of biometric data to third parties. Your data is processed securely and anonymously.
